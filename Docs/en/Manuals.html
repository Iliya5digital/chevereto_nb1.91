Chevereto manuals

These manuals are made so you can count with custom tools for your new image hosting. You can also collaborate in this section of with your own manual or make improvements to the current ones. Please visit our forum for more info.

Bookmarklet

With one click lets you view all the images in a given web page, then with a simple click you can host the images on yout new image hosting. See the Bookmarklet manual.

Firefox Extension

This Firefox extension allows you to add two items to the context menu of an image, which allows you to upload this picture to your image hosting or upload and resize it. See the Firefox extension manual

Hotlinkig Manual

Allows yo to determine a list of domains that can directly show your images, preventing the bandwidth theft. See the Hotlinking manual
